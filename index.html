What is Claude?

Claude is an AI assistant created by Anthropic, designed to be helpful, harmless, and honest. For marketing teams, Claude offers advanced capabilities including long-form content creation, strategic analysis, brand voice consistency, and the ability to create actual files like presentations, spreadsheets, and documents directly.

What does this mean practically? You can upload your entire brand guidelines, product catalog, and campaign history into a single conversation—Claude will remember and reference all of it. You can ask for a 50-page report and get it in one response. And with Microsoft 365 connected, Claude can search your SharePoint, email, and Teams without you uploading anything.

Why Claude for Marketing?

Content at Scale
Generate product descriptions, social posts, email campaigns, and long-form content while maintaining consistent brand voice across all channels.
Strategic Analysis
Analyze competitor positioning, market trends, and campaign performance with deep research capabilities that pull real-time data.
Global Localization
Adapt campaigns for international markets with culturally-aware translations that preserve brand intent and messaging nuance.

Prompting Guide

How to get the best results from Claude

The Prompting Mindset

Claude isn't a search engine or a magic box—it's more like a very capable colleague who needs clear direction. The more context and specificity you provide, the better your results will be.

Best Practices

Be Specific
Include word counts, format requirements, tone descriptions. "Write a blog post" is weak; "Write a 1,200-word blog post with 5 sections" is strong.
Provide Context
Share relevant background: who is the audience, what's the goal, where will this appear?
Give Examples
Show Claude what good looks like. Upload examples, paste samples, or describe the style to match.
Iterate
First drafts are starting points. Ask for revisions. "Make it more conversational" or "Shorten by 30%" are valid follow-ups.

Prompt Template

Structure Framework
ROLE: [Who should Claude be? e.g., "You are a senior copywriter at TaylorMade"] TASK: [What do you need? Be specific about deliverable] CONTEXT: [Background information, audience, goals, constraints] FORMAT: [Structure, length, style requirements] EXAMPLES: [What good looks like, or what to avoid] OUTPUT: [Exactly what you want back—file type, sections, etc.]

What NOT to Do

  • Being too vague: "Write something about golf" gives Claude nothing to work with
  • Skipping context: Claude doesn't know your campaign history unless you share it
  • One-and-done thinking: Iteration almost always produces better results
  • Ignoring format: Specify exactly what you want back

Brand Voice

Maintaining TaylorMade's voice across AI-generated content

Why Voice Consistency Matters

AI can generate content quickly, but consistency is what builds brand recognition. Every piece of content—whether written by a human or AI—should feel unmistakably TaylorMade.

TaylorMade Voice Attributes

Confident
We speak with authority earned through decades of innovation and tour success. We make bold claims—and back them up.
Passionate
Genuine enthusiasm for golf and equipment innovation. We love what we do, and that energy comes through.
Innovative
Forward-thinking and technology-focused. We're always pushing boundaries and bringing new ideas to the game.

Measuring Success

Establish Your Baseline First

Before rolling out Claude, document your current state so you can measure actual improvement:

  • Time per deliverable: How long does it take to write a product description? A blog post?
  • Content volume: How many pieces does your team produce per week/month?
  • Revision cycles: How many rounds of edits before content is approved?
  • Agency spend: What do you currently outsource and at what cost?

Track These Metrics Post-Implementation

  • Time savings: Compare time-to-completion for similar deliverables
  • Output increase: Measure change in content volume with same team size
  • Quality consistency: Track brand voice adherence and approval rates
  • Cost impact: Document any reduction in agency or contractor spend
  • Team adoption: Track active users and usage patterns
Set Realistic Expectations

Results vary by use case. Some tasks (like generating social post variations) see immediate efficiency gains. Others (like strategic documents) may see quality improvements rather than time savings. Measure what matters for your specific workflows.
